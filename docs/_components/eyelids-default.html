Eyelids is an innovative web component that can be useful to the people who read a lot on the computer screen. It helps you focus on the text or other content in cases of pages with a long chunks of text, or even to help cover up the distracting parts of the complex web page. To use it, just hit Ctrl + 1 on the keyboard. The eyelids will close down vertically on your mouse position and will follow its movement allowing you a clean horizontal area for better focus on the content. To dismiss it, just hit Ctrl + 1 again or Esc key.

Eyelids has the capability to adjust the width of the highlighted area on the screen. To do that, press Shift key while Eyelids is active and scroll with your mousewheel.

Optionally, you can add the eyelids notifier on the page with the following code:

category: innovative type: eyelids order: 1 ---
